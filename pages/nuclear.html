Nuclear Winter

The environment has now shifted. Following the lead of the previous grungy textures, we are now deeper in the bunker, but the warm light is faint, struggling against a pervasive, icy blue ambient glow. The ground is frost-rimed, and the setting is bleak. On a frozen shelf, replacing the previous meal, is a rough clay bowl containing the glowing, unnaturally vibrant green Irradiated Moss & Mushroom Broth. Next to it, on a shard of slate, rest several dark, black-crusted strips of Charcoal-Cured Jerky, covered in a fine layer of gray ash. The scene is cold, isolated, and desolate.

Irradiated Moss & Mushroom Broth

  • Bioluminescent Moss: Deep cave or bunker-grown glowing flora for that distinct neon-green hue.
  • Wild Cave Mushrooms: Sliced thinly to add texture to the liquid.
  • Melted Frost Water: Pure rehydrated ice runoff.

Charcoal-Cured Jerky

  • Mystery Protein Strips: Lean strips of whatever wildlife survived the fallout.
  • Campfire Ash & Slate Salt: Used to heavily encrust and dry-cure the meat for long-term storage.
